Ubuntu is a widely-used open-source operating system based on the Linux kernel. It is developed and maintained by Canonical Ltd. and is known for its ease of use and strong focus on user experience. Ubuntu comes in several editions, including Desktop, Server, and Core (for IoT devices), making it versatile for various applications. It's popular among both beginners and experienced users in the Linux community due to its accessibility, comprehensive support, and robust security features. Ubuntu's commitment to free software principles and its reliable performance has made it a preferred choice for many users around the world.
